## 1. VERIZON

**Lowest-Cost Unlimited Plan:** **Unlimited Welcome — $65/mo** (1 line, with AutoPay & paper-free billing). Without AutoPay the price is **$75/mo**. A **$10/line/month AutoPay + paper-free billing discount** applies.

**Data Limits / Throttling Policy:** Unlimited data on Verizon's network. On the Unlimited Welcome plan, video streams at 480p (SD) and data may be deprioritized (slowed) during network congestion compared to premium-tier customers. The higher-tier plans (Unlimited Plus at $80/mo and Unlimited Ultimate at $90/mo with AutoPay) offer higher-priority data.

**Hotspot Allowance:**
- **Unlimited Welcome**: No mobile hotspot included (hotspot not available or only at 600 Kbps)
- **Unlimited Plus**: 30 GB high-speed hotspot per month
- **Unlimited Ultimate**: 60 GB high-speed hotspot per month

**International Roaming / Texting Benefits:**
- **Unlimited Welcome**: No meaningful international roaming included
- **Unlimited Plus**: Talk, text & data in Mexico & Canada; limited international coverage
- **Unlimited Ultimate**: Talk, text & data in Mexico & Canada; international travel benefits included

**Streaming Perks / Included Subscriptions:**
- **Unlimited Welcome**: No streaming perks included
- **Unlimited Plus**: +Apple One, or Disney+/Hulu/ESPN+ bundle (as an add-on perk option)
- **Unlimited Ultimate**: Includes top-tier perks such as Apple One or Netflix + an entertainment subscription bundle

**AutoPay Discount Requirements:** **$10/line/month** discount applied when enrolled in AutoPay AND paper-free (paperless) billing. The advertised price ($65/mo for Welcome, 1 line) already reflects this discount.

**Activation Fees / Hidden Monthly Fees:**
- **Activation fee**: **$40 per line** (one-time, per Broadband Facts)
- **Regulatory recovery fee** and government taxes are added on top of the advertised monthly price
- No annual contract required
- Prices shown are before taxes and surcharges

**Lowest-Cost Unlimited Plan:** **AT&T Unlimited Starter / Extra 2.0** — approximately **$65/mo for 1 line** with AutoPay (prices for 1 line are higher than the 4-line per-line rates advertised prominently).
